Garage Manager

Shop management software

Overview

After working in the auto-repair industry for multiple years, it quickly came apparent that organization of data was crucial to running a successful shop. From customer and vehicle records to repair orders and the different vendors we work with, it all needed to be stored in an efficient manner such that retrieving what you were looking for took seconds.

That was the inspiration behind designing and building this project, the solution we needed to solve all of our problems.
